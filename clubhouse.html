Clubhouse: XR Family & Haptics Club


A little bit of history

I joined Clubhouse back in October 2020 after having been a long time lurker since its private release around March 2020.
I started hosting casual rooms focused on the topics of Augmented / Mixed / Virtual Reality (AR/MR/VR), and even accomodated different timezones for users from Europe, Africa and Asia.
Fast forward to 2021, and I started inviting guests from the industry to come share their learnings and experiences with the audience on a weekly basis.

The XR Family was born.


The goal was to explore the vast diversity of industries in which XR had an impact.
We had topics around the AR Cloud, 3D Avatars, Mental Health, Immersive Theatre, Education, Volumetric Capture, Haptics, and VR Gaming to only name a few.

I invited the audience to participate to live Q&A sessions with interesting speakers such as:
- Timmu Tõke (CEO & Co-Founder of Wolf3D)
- Ferhan Ozkan (Co-Founder of XR Bootcamp)
- Mar Gonzalez Franco (Principal researcher at Microsoft Research)
...and many more.

The community grew very fast, and in April 2021 we reached over 9000 followers & members!
That's when Eric Vezzoli (CEO & Founder of Interhaptics) reached out to me.
We decided to use that momentum to start a monthly podcast with guests from the haptic industry to help grow the awareness around this technology.

We teamed up with other haptic experts such as:
- Ashley Huffman (Strategic Alliances at Nano Magnetics)
- Gijs den Butter (CEO & Co-Founder of SenseGlove)
- Irina Tripapina (Lead Marketer at SenseGlove)
- Sarah Alkibsy (Marketing Representative at Interhaptics)

The Haptics Club was born.


Wether you're just curious or an expert in these fields, you're welcome here.
This is a safe place for everyone.
