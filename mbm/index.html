Features

Rules

Designed by manu.vision.

• Red eats Green, Green eats Blue, Blue eats Red.

• RGB organisms flock, chase prey, flee predators, and eat matching food pellets.

• White food is gone in this export; food drops inherit the killed organism’s color.

• The yellow boss hunts everyone and decays over time.

• Static currents only move food pellets and are randomized on reset.

• Trails are long, shrinking, and fade with organism decay.

• The boss auto-spawns when too much food accumulates, or you can spawn it manually.
