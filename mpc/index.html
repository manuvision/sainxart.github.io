Quickstart cards — from the box
▶
Get started making beats
1
Turn up Main Volume and tap pads to play the default kit in Sample mode.
2
Press PLAY to hear a full sequence with those sounds.
3
Press SEQ to open Sequence Mode. Tap lit pads during playback to switch beats.
Tip: Double-press STOP rapidly to kill all sound instantly.
🔊
Explore 100s of sounds
1
Tap a pad, then press SAMPLE SELECT.
2
Turn the Encoder to browse. Each sample previews as you scroll. Push to load.
3
Press SEQ then PLAY to hear your new sound in the beat.
⏺
Record your own sounds
1
Press SAMPLE RECORD.
2
Tap a pad to start. The waveform builds in real time.
3
Tap the pad again or press STOP to finish.
Tip: Turn K1 to change source: Mic, USB (phone), or Rear (line-in).
✂️
Adjust & trim your sample
1
In Sample Mode: K1 = start point, K2 = end point.
2
Hold SHIFT + PAD 13 (TRIM SAMPLE) to permanently cut outside the markers.
3
Press CHOP to slice across pads. Press LOOP to loop the sample.
Tip: See ⇧ on screen? Hold SHIFT to zoom the waveform with the knobs for precise trimming.
⏺
Record a new sequence
1
In Sequence Mode, tap an unlit pad to select an empty slot.
2
Press SEQ RECORD to arm recording.
3
Press PLAY. Tap pads to lay down your beat. Loops and overdubs automatically.
4
Press STOP when done. Press PLAY again to listen back.
Tip: Made a mistake? Hold SHIFT + − to undo the last recording pass instantly.
✨
Powerful FX
1
Press PAD FX and hit pads to trigger effects. Harder press = more effect.
2
Press KNOB FX to control one effect with K1–K3, keeping pads free.
3
Press SHIFT + PAD FX to enter Flex Beat — time-warp stutter effects.
Tip: Press SHIFT + KNOB FX to choose which effect and which pads it applies to.
