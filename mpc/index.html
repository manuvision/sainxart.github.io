Quickstart cards — from the box
▶
Get started making beats
1
Turn up Main Volume and tap pads to play the default kit in Sample mode.
2
Press PLAY to hear a full sequence with those sounds.
3
Press SEQ to open Sequence Mode. Tap lit pads during playback to switch between beats.
Tip: Double-press STOP rapidly to kill all sound immediately. Your emergency reset.
🔊
Explore 100s of sounds
1
Tap a pad, then press SAMPLE SELECT.
2
Turn the Encoder to browse. Each sample previews as you scroll. Push to load it to the pad.
3
Press SEQ then PLAY to hear your new sound in the beat.
⏺
Record your own sounds
1
Press SAMPLE RECORD.
2
Tap a pad to start recording. The waveform builds in real time.
3
Press STOP (or tap the pad again) to finish.
Tip: Turn K1 on the Sample Record page to change source: Mic (built-in), USB (phone), or Rear (line-in).
✂
Adjust & trim your sample
1
In Sample Mode, turn K1 for start point, K2 for end point.
2
Hold SHIFT + PAD 13 (TRIM SAMPLE) to permanently cut everything outside the markers.
3
Press CHOP to slice across pads. Press LOOP to loop the sample.
Tip: See the ⇧ icon on screen? Hold SHIFT to access extra options — including waveform zoom using the knobs for precise trimming.
▶●
Record a new sequence
1
In Sequence Mode, tap an unlit pad to select an empty sequence slot.
2
Press SEQ RECORD to arm recording.
3
Press PLAY. Tap pads to lay down your beat. The sequence loops and overdubs automatically.
4
Press STOP when done. Press PLAY again to listen back.
Tip: Made a mistake? Hold SHIFT + press − to instantly undo all events from the last recording pass.
✨
Powerful FX
1
Press PAD FX and hit pads to trigger effects. Harder press = more effect applied.
2
Press KNOB FX to control one effect with K1–K3, keeping pads free for samples.
3
Press SHIFT + PAD FX to enter Flex Beat — time-warp stutter and scratch effects.
Tip: Press SHIFT + KNOB FX to open FX select — choose the effect and which pads it applies to.

The boom bap workflow
"I don't sample to be lazy. I sample to have a conversation with the past. Find the moment that moves you — then make it yours."
— Inspired by Madlib & J Dilla
How the greats would use the MPC Sample
1
Find your source
Play jazz, soul, or funk from your phone via USB. Look for chords, a horn stab, or a 2-bar loop that hits you.
2
Sample Record — grab the moment
SAMPLE RECORD → K1 select USB. Wait for the right bar. Tap a pad to capture 4–8 seconds. Tap again to stop.
3
Trim it tight
SAMPLE mode: K1 = start, K2 = end. Right at the first hit. Then SHIFT + PAD 13 to permanently cut outside the markers.
4
Chop into slices
CHOP → Regions 4 or 8. Play the slices. Pick 2–4 that sound musical. The rest: ignore them.
5
Tune and filter each slice
B2 → Semi Tune (K1): try +5, +7, +12. B3 → Classic filter, Cutoff ~65, Resonance ~20. Warm, dusty MPC sound.
6
Load drums on Bank A
PAD BANK → Bank A. Kick → PAD 1, Snare → PAD 2, Hi-hat → PAD 3. Use built-in BoomBap kit or your own recordings.
7
Set tempo: 85–90 BPM
SEQ → hold B1 + turn Encoder. 85–90 BPM is boom bap sweet spot. Press PLAY to hear the pulse.
8
Record in layers
SEQ RECORD → PLAY. Loop 1: kick + snare. Loop 2: add hi-hats. Loop 3: switch Bank B, add chop slices. Build up gradually.
9
Use Note Repeat for hi-hat rolls
Hold NOTE REPEAT + tap PAD 3. B1/B2/B3 set division (1/8, 1/16, 1/32). Hold SHIFT for triplets — instant shuffle feel.
10
Glue with the compressor
SHIFT + PAD 5. Amount ~60%. Color ON for tape warmth. This punches everything together into one cohesive beat.
11
Export your beat
SHIFT + PAD 12 (SONG). Insert your sequence. Export → Audio Mixdown. Transfer via USB to your phone.
