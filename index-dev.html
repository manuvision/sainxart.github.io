Start with Why
“People don’t buy WHAT you do; they buy WHY you do it.”
This is a famous quote from Simon Sinek's TED Talk:
"Start With Why"
To build trust with your audience, you need authenticity. It means that your WHAT (actions) have to be consistent and in harmony with your WHY (values). People can detect dissonances, which may affect their trust.
