Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

PopColor is a simple arcade puzzle game by Manu.Vision. This privacy policy explains what information the game handles and how it is used.

Information We Collect

PopColor does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or other personal information.

The game may store gameplay information locally on your device, such as settings, high scores, leaderboard initials, and game preferences. This information stays on your device and is used only to make the game work.

Analytics and Tracking

PopColor does not use third-party advertising, cross-app tracking, or behavioral analytics. We do not sell personal information.

Data Sharing

We do not share PopColor gameplay data with third parties. If the app is downloaded through an app store, Apple or Google may process purchase, download, crash, and diagnostic information according to their own privacy policies and user settings.

Children's Privacy

PopColor is designed as a casual game and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Deletion

Because PopColor stores gameplay data locally, you can remove that data by using in-game reset options where available or by deleting the app from your device.

Changes to This Policy

If PopColor's privacy practices change, this page will be updated with a new effective date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through manu.vision.
