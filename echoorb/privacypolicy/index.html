Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

EchoOrb is an iOS app by Manu.Vision that transforms sounds, recordings, audio files, and videos into 3D sound signatures with visuals and haptics. This privacy policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

Information We Collect

EchoOrb does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, or other personal information.

EchoOrb may store app information locally on your device, such as settings, imported media references, recordings you create, source crop choices, generated sound analysis data, and export preferences. This information stays on your device and is used only to make the app work.

Microphone, Photos, and Files

EchoOrb asks for microphone access only when you choose to record audio. It asks for Photos or file access only when you choose to import media or save an export. Audio, video, image, and recording data is processed locally on your device and is not uploaded to Manu.Vision servers.

Purchases

EchoOrb+ is a one-time in-app purchase handled by Apple through the App Store. Manu.Vision does not receive your payment card information. Apple may process purchase and transaction information according to Apple's privacy policy and your App Store settings.

Analytics and Tracking

EchoOrb does not use third-party advertising, cross-app tracking, or behavioral analytics. We do not sell personal information.

Data Sharing

We do not share EchoOrb recordings, imports, exports, or sound analysis data with third parties. If the app is downloaded through the App Store, Apple may process purchase, download, crash, and diagnostic information according to its own privacy policies and user settings.

Children's Privacy

EchoOrb is designed as a creative sound visualization tool and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Deletion

Because EchoOrb stores app data locally, you can remove recordings, imported media, and generated data using in-app deletion controls where available or by deleting the app from your device.

Changes to This Policy

If EchoOrb's privacy practices change, this page will be updated with a new effective date.

Contact
