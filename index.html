Bio / Manuel Sainsily

Manuel Sainsily is a Creative Director who designs, teaches, and speaks about the intersection of art, technology, and culture, with a focus on world-building and storytelling. Born in Guadeloupe and based in Montreal, he has spent more than 15 years building products and emerging technologies at IBM, Immersion, Unity, and Adobe, where his work spans artificial intelligence, XR, haptics, and design systems.

A TED speaker and guest lecturer at Harvard, MIT, Oxford, Stanford, Columbia, McGill, and other leading universities, Manuel co-created MasterClass's first Generative AI course in 2024, now among the platform's five most-watched courses. His work exploring cultural heritage preservation, real-time 3D, and brain-computer interfaces has reached more than 20 million people through immersive exhibitions, films, and interactive experiences.

Recognized with the Mozilla Rise25 Award, Manuel advises organizations and audiences worldwide on designing a more human-centered future.
